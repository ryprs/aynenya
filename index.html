About the game Voidwrought is a fast-paced action-platformer Metroidvania that brings fresh air to the genre with sharp controls, immersive exploration, and a deep story. The arrival of the Crimson Star marks a new era. Emerging from its cocoon, the Entity is determined to collect the Ichor — the blood of the gods — from the hoarding abominations. Players explore the multidimensional depths beneath the star-scorched surface, discovering the decadent Palace, the icy tunnels of the Ancient Waters, and the tragic fate of the Forsaken Expedition. You can collect and equip over 30 powerful Artifacts and Souls — from spectral weapons to passive enhancements — to customize your playstyle. In place of the ruined Grey City, you can build a temple filled with loyal cultists. Dig through temple corridors, carve up the corpses of fallen gods, and hunt in hidden cosmic corners to gather unique powers and treasures sought by scholars, the brave, and the mad. Voidwrought offers intricate maneuvers, diverse abilities, and challenging boss battles in a rich, atmospheric world..
